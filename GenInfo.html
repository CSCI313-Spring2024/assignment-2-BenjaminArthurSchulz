My name is Benjamin A Schulz, I was born on August 1st, 2001 6 weeks early, and on my Grandpa's birthday no less so that's cool, I grew up
in a small town called Jamestown in North Dakota and I lived there most of my life, only moving to Fargo recently to attend classes at NDSU.
Here are a few fun facts about my life, When I was in the 1st grade on November 11th (don't ask me how I remember the exact day) my gloved
hands slipped of the icy monkey bars and I landed on my arm breaking the two bones that make up my forearm, and the administrators and the
Catholic school I attended put me in the Teacher's lounge while they called my mom to ask her to take me to the hospital. Instead of laying
me on the nice soft couch that was in there they took some chairs that were around a table, stuck em together, and had me lay on that. I was
in gymnastics for a few years after a friend had their birthday party at the special gym we have for it in town, but I quit because I was one
of very few boys, and it always felt like the people working there were parading me around, I kinda wish I stuck with it though would've been
really cool to get good at a lot of those tricks. The only other sports I really participated in were Soccer and Cross-Country, my dad was a
Soccer coach so that was a given but I also really enjoyed Cross-Country, my only problem was how horrible the courses at competitions were.

I am a Computer Science major mainly because when in High School the only class I enjoyed that was a feesible career path was a Programming
class I took junior year, and with the industry growing as it is it looked like a pretty safe option. I don't have many aspirations outside of wanting

can always go back to working at Domino's for a bit to save up some money and figure my life. Although if I do want to get into the industry
